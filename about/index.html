Tao LIN is an Austrian-Chinese Designer born in 1986 in Chengdu, China. In 2000 he moved to Vienna, Austria with his family. He studied in Switzerland and later in 2014, he received his master’s degree in visual communication from ZHdK (Zurich University of the Arts). With a rigorous conceptual and typographic approach, his practice ranges from editorial design to visual identity, packaging and websites etc. His works received several national and international design awards. He commutes between Shanghai and Europe.

Other than design work, he is also active in the art education industry under the name Class Tao. His students received offers from top notch schools like Yale, RISD, Royal Academy of Art in London etc.

    Field of expertise:
  • Visual Identity
  • Type & Typography
  • Editorial Design
  • Book Design
  • Packaging Design
  • Digital Design
